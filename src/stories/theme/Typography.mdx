自定义字体配置生成

```ts
$custom-typography: mat-typography-config(
  $font-family: $font-family,
  $display-4: mat-typography-level(61px, 1.5, 300, $font-family: $font-family),
  $display-3: mat-typography-level(48px, 1.5, 400, $font-family: $font-family),
  $display-2: mat-typography-level(39px, 1.5, 400, $font-family: $font-family),
  $display-1: mat-typography-level(31px, 1.5, 400, $font-family: $font-family),
  $headline: mat-typography-level(25px, 1.618, 400, $font-family: $font-family),
  $title: mat-typography-level(20px, 1.618, 500, $font-family: $font-family),
  $body-1: mat-typography-level(16px, 1.618, 400),
  $body-2: mat-typography-level(16px, 1.618, 500),
);
```
